InSignEx

Embedded Developer | Gujarat, India | May 2020 - Jun 2021
Embedded Systems Sensors Python Flask

Project Overview

InSignEx is an embedded hardware project focused on precision agriculture. I developed an intelligent, automated irrigation system capable of monitoring crop health in real-time, allowing for highly efficient water usage and targeted nutrient distribution.

The Challenge

In the Gujarat region of India, water supplies are often unreliable and fluctuate weekly, making it difficult for farmers to consistently water and fertilize crops. Furthermore, farms require cost-effective solutions capable of scaling across acres of land, even in areas where internet connectivity may be unreliable.

The Solution

The solution integrates a fertigation system with an app-based monitoring platform. This provides easy access to data and visualizations, helping users quickly understand trends regarding plant growth, soil absorption, and mineral saturation.

Technical Implementations

  • Low Power Mode: Conducted a power analysis on the ESP32 to minimize power consumption during idle periods, utilizing external clocks to trigger system actions.
  • Logic Control: Wrote embedded C++ code to interact with a Python-based Flask server, transmitting sensor data back to a centralized system.
  • Data Visualization: Developed a preliminary application allowing users to easily view data trends, adjust fertilizer and watering schedules, and spot anomalies.
