Project Overview

Teach4Charity is a community-driven initiative I organized to leverage the academic strengths of high school students for social good. By establishing a student-led tutoring network, we provided high-quality educational support to younger students while raising funds for critical charitable causes.

The Mission

I noticed two distinct community needs: local elementary students lacked access to engaging, affordable STEM tutoring, and highly capable high school peers were looking for meaningful ways to volunteer and give back to their community.

The Impact

By bridging this gap, the initiative successfully raised over $4,000 for charity. These funds were used directly to provide the gift of sight to over 100 people and to supply essential STEM materials and kits to local underfunded elementary schools.

Key Contributions

  • Program Management: Organized the logistics of matching high school tutors with elementary students, creating a sustainable schedule and tracking volunteer hours.
  • Curriculum Development: Helped guide the creation of approachable, engaging STEM curriculum specifically designed to foster an early interest in science, technology, and math.
  • Fund Allocation: Managed the financial logistics of the program, ensuring 100% of the proceeds were securely transferred to partner charities and used to purchase educational materials.
