About Me

I am an engineer focused on turning technology and robotics into real social value. I like hard, open-ended problems and I enjoy learning across fields to solve them. I work best in positive, collaborative teams and I try to bring a practical mindset, clear communication, and steady execution to every project.

I started by tinkering at home, then in 7th grade a Raspberry Pi pulled me deeper into computer engineering. My first bigger project was a robot that navigated the house to deliver drinks to my parents. Since then I have kept raising the bar, from robots that detect microbes to digital design work and a small satellite project, all of which strengthened my creativity, systems thinking, and problem-solving skills.
